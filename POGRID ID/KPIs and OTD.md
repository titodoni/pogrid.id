# KPIs and OTD

The metrics that tell the Owner whether the floor is healthy.

- **OTD Rate** — On-Time Delivery rate (the headline metric)
- **Manufactured Volume** — output over the period
- **Lead Time** — time from order to delivery

All three show **period-over-period comparison** (Current Month vs Previous Month), always visible on the Owner Matrix Dashboard.

## Interaction
KPI cards, stage metrics, and the Client Scoreboard are clickable — clicking applies a global filter to the directory below. Directory pill filters: Per Client (default), Marked (Rework/Trouble), Delayed, On Time, Close Due Date.
