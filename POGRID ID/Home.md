# POGrid Wiki Home

> Knowledge hub for the **POgrid.id** Obsidian vault.
> Start here, then follow [[wikilinks]] to any concept. Every concept note links back here — that is the bi-directional web.

POgrid.id is a **Live Progress & Delivery Punctuality Tracker** for Indonesian SME manufacturing workshops. It is **NOT** an ERP, MES, inventory, or accounting tool. Its one job: *"Where is my order right now, and will it be on time?"*

## Core Concepts

- [[The Soul]] — the product's purpose and what it refuses to be
- [[Core Paradigm]] — Item-Centric, Zero-Block, Asynchronous Progress Tracking
- [[Tech Stack]] — Laravel 11 · React 18 · Inertia v2 · Tailwind v4 · Neon Postgres · Pusher
- [[Workflow Stages]] — Drafter → Purchasing → Productions → QC → Finance → Delivery
- [[Roles and Access]] — Owner, Admin, Worker
- [[Auth and Onboarding]] — Office password guard + Worker PIN guard
- [[KPIs and OTD]] — On-Time Delivery, Manufactured Volume, Lead Time
- [[Real-Time and Pusher]] — WebSocket timeline alerts, no polling
- [[Hosting and Deployment]] — Hostinger shared + Neon, cron-only background work
